Magnolia

Magnolia grandiflora, commonly known as the Southern magnolia or bull bay, is a tree of the family Magnoliaceae native to the southeastern United States, from coastal Virginia south to central Florida, and west to eastern Texas and Oklahoma. Reaching 27.5m (90 ft) in height, it is a large striking evergreen tree with large dark green leaves and large white fragrant flowers. Widely, widely, cultivated.
